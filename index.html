College Information For Undocumented Students

Colgate University:

Question: Does this institute consider undocumented students as international students?

Answer: Colgate does review undocumented applicants as foreign national or international in their process.

Question: Does this institute consider undocumented students as domestic appicants?

Answer: At this time, no.

Question: Does this college meet full financial need for undocumented students?

Answer: Colgate meets 100% of demonstrated need for all admitted students, including international/ DACA/ undocumented students.

Question: Is the college application process need aware or need blind for undocumented students?

Answer: Need aware

Question: Are there on campus resources/opportunities for undocumented students?

Answer: Undocumented students have access to all of the same resources as any Colgate student.

Question: Does the college have an undocumented student advisor?

Answer: Colgate's director for the Office of International Student Services (OISS) is the administrative dean (advisor) for these students.

Question: Is the campus a sanctuary campus/city?

Answer: Colgate has not formally announced itself as a sanctuary campus. However, Colgate joined the other New York Six Liberal Arts colleges and universities in declaring support for undocumented individuals.

Question: Is there a DACA and undocumented organication on campus?

Answer: No
